About us

Our company was founded in 2009. We are a major producer and exporter of dried fruits in Kyrgyzstan. Today KYRGYZ FRUIT is one of the leaders in the Kyrgyz market for the production and sale of biologically pure, and therefore produced from naturally grown raw materials, dried fruits. We export our products to all Western European countries. The largest volumes of supplies are carried out to countries such as Great Britain, Germany, Israel.

All products are manufactured on high quality equipment. The raisins are laser processed on VISYS equipment and sorted by color and size. The quality of our products meets all international standards, as they are produced under sterile conditions from environmentally friendly raw materials. Competent work of qualified personnel, excellent knowledge of agricultural business and the desires of consumers allows our company to rapidly and dynamically develop in the world market.
